How to read these journeys. Each person — an undergraduate, a graduate student, or a postdoc — is shown as a path: where they studied before UNR → their role and the years they were here → where they are now. A red “runs their own lab” badge links to that person’s lab; role and profile links (LinkedIn, Google Scholar, academic pages) appear under each name where available. Everything comes from public lab and alumni pages; where a lab doesn’t publish something, we say so or show an initials avatar rather than guess.
